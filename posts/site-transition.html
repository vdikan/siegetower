Site Transition

Tagged as en, emacs, future
Written on 2021-09-28

I've been switching recently to org-roam for the notes/ wiki/ agenda catalogs in emacs, and realized that half of this page content is tangled from .org-files scattered all over the machines (on a synchronized partition, of course).

It would be then nice and only logical to move the whole site generator to emacs org-mode. Parsers already there, templating engines too. Most of the effort will be spent on index building, which sounds like a fun exercise.

So from now on and indefinitely this page is under transition. Things gonna move around and (dis)appear randomly.
