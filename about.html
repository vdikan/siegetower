the Hollow Siege Tower -- {About}

Square Sun shines from the sky upon the desolate Land of Lisp, where the hollow hull of an Ivory Siege Tower stands still. Its magic engine is silent, birds nest in its catapult on the roof. Parts of the construct are being salvaged by the sturdy inhabitants of the desert.

At a construction site nearby a new Tower is being built. Desert winds reflecting from its sharp angles sing a song songs of swiftness and power. Some other day the new construct will roam the wilds.

But now it is under construction.
